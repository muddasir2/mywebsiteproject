1.4 Describe the risks that might impact digital projects and what causes their failure
A failure to plan and prepare will certainly increase the risk of projects becoming rushed and incomplete, Even in the process of coding the project, if code is rushed / unclear, it can quickly become messy, leading to more room for bugs and mistakes. If a digital project is worked on by more than 1 person, this also increases a risk of dysfunctional code, because when the code is integrated into a single project or file, there may be compatibility issues, there is also a higher margin for error the more users there are working on a project.
